Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Who We Are

MagicSteam is a local Magic: The Gathering card marketplace based in Oman, operated as a community platform for buying and selling cards among local players. Contact us at muhannad.abri@gmail.com for any privacy-related questions or requests.

2. Data We Collect

Account data — your email address, username, and hashed password. We never store your password in plain text.

Activity data — when you log in or use the site, we record your IP address, approximate login timestamp, and browser/device type. This is used to understand how the platform is used and to detect suspicious account activity.

Listing data — cards you list for sale, including price, condition, and notes you provide.

3. Why We Collect It

  • To operate the marketplace and manage your account
  • To detect and prevent unauthorized access or fraudulent listings
  • To understand the geographic and demographic composition of our user base, which helps us improve the platform and tailor it to the Oman MTG community
  • To maintain activity logs that allow us to investigate disputes or reports

Our legal basis is legitimate interest under Oman's Personal Data Protection Law (Royal Decree 6/2022), specifically for the purposes of service operation, security, and first-party analytics.

4. Location Data from IP Addresses

IP addresses collected during login and site use may be resolved to an approximate country and city using a locally-hosted geolocation database. This data is used solely for internal analytics (e.g. understanding what cities our users come from) and is never shared with third parties or used for targeted advertising.

5. How Long We Keep It

  • Account data: retained while your account is active
  • Activity logs (IP, timestamps): retained for 12 months, then deleted
  • Listing and order history: retained indefinitely for record-keeping

6. Who We Share It With

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. All data is stored locally on our own server infrastructure. If payment processing is enabled in the future, only the minimum data required by the payment provider (Tap Payments or Stripe) will be shared, and only for the purpose of completing a transaction.

7. Your Rights (Oman PDPL)

Under Oman's Personal Data Protection Law you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated personal data
  • Object to processing of your data for analytics purposes

To exercise any of these rights, email muhannad.abri@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies & Local Storage

MagicSteam does not use tracking cookies. We use your browser's localStorage to store your login token and your sell cart on your own device. This data never leaves your device except as part of authenticated API requests to our server.

9. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes materially we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the platform after a policy update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

MagicSteam — Not affiliated with or endorsed by Wizards of the Coast. Questions? muhannad.abri@gmail.com
